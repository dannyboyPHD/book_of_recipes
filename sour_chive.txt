Description
Sour Cream and Chive Dip is perfect for dunking into with chips and vegetables!

Ingredients
* 500g Sour cream
* 3 Tbsp fresh chives (minced)
* 2 Ends of green onions (chopped)
* 2 Tsp Onion powder
* 1/2 Tsp Salt

Directions
* Mix all ingredients
* Refrigerate for atleast 12 hrs
* Serve with crisps/nachos
